Algowls

Ride across the AVL trees and accomplish the quest of balancing. Use Dijkstra and find the shortest path to the treasure. Use algorithms to unravel the mysteries of the question mark. Dynamic programming will be your light that will help you survive. Read the map of Matrix and follow the stars aligned in Complex Graphs to reach the final level of solution. But remember to carry your sword of optimization all along.

Presenting to you all Algowls- "striving for solutions using algorithms".


Instructions:
● The teams will be given a paper containing 20 MCQs and 1 subjective type question.
● The teams will be allotted 40 minutes to answer the paper.
● MCQs are divided into three sections: 10 questions carrying 1 mark each with no negative marking. 5 questions of 2 marks each with 0.5 negative marks. 5 questions of 3 marks
    each with 1 negative mark.
● The MCQ paper will be based on algorithms and its analysis.
● Subjective question carries 10 marks.
● The subjective question will be based on a story and the story will move around a set of problems, grouped in parts.
● Problems lying in story may or may not be well known algorithms.
● The evaluation of subjective question will be done on the basis of feasibility, optimality, running time complexity and space complexity of the solutions.
● In case of tie, team with more 3 marker correct questions will be the winner. If it's also same, then the one with more 2 marker correct questions will be chosen. At last if all things
    are same coin will be tossed.

Rules and regulations:
● It is a team based event. Each team can have a maximum of 2 members. Lone wolves are also welcome.
● Use of Internet, pen-drives or any sort of helping material is strictly.
● All the teams must be present at the specified room/lab on time.
● The decision of the organizers shall be final and binding. Organizers have the right to disqualify any team if they break any rules.

Allow the capabilities of a coder inside you come forth!!!! Let the coder flow freely!!!
Do you consider yourself as the one who thrives for efficiency and wants to be prolific in coding, one who can give the quickest and easiest solution of a problem?
Then, here's your territory!!!!

Round 1

1. It will be containing concept Based MCQ, One word and One Line questions.
2. The duration of this round will be of 45 minutes in which 30 questions have to be answered.
3. It will be divided into 2 sections, each of equal weightage. Section 1 will be based on Java and Section 2 will be based on C++.
4. 10 teams with the highest scores will progress to next round.

3. Programmers will have only 1 hour for these questions.
4. Marks will be rewarded for each correct question at the end of event.

Code Benders

This will be an online overnight coding contest hosted on CodeChef, of three hours duration. This is not a team event and there won’t be any separate rounds, just one main coding round, that’s it.

There are no restrictions on participation, so any individual can participate. One just needs to have a CodeChef account which can be created here – https://www.codechef.com/signup. (One can also login using Google, Facebook or GitHub account)

Prize money will only be given to the top Indian student performer.

RULES:

1. There will be 5 – 6 problems (of varying difficulty levels) which will be logical, mathematical and algorithmic in nature.
2. Participants can write solution to a problem in any programming language of their choice. Their code should produce the correct answer and adhere to the constraints given for a
    problem, in order to get an ACCEPTED verdict.
3. Contest format will be similar to IOI, and so will be the rank list style (IOI Style (https://www.codechef.com/rankings#IOIStyleRanklist)).
4. The result will be declared promptly after the contest, or on the next day.
5. Decisions made by the event organizers will be final and binding. Any arguments or nuisance will not be tolerated.

Start Time: 09:00 PM (31st January 2020)
End Time: 12:00 AM (01st February 2020)
Duration: 3 Hours

Event Head:

Himanshu Singal (B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Science, 3rd Year)

Snake Jam

You may fall, you may rise.. But you win only if you are wise;

Roll the dice, the snake-ladder waits.. Not just plain lady luck, but a stroke of knowledge and fate;
Things get tricky here..
If you are lucky, even the snake might help you.

Event Description:

Snake Jam is a non-tech fun event. Even if the participants are unaware of technical things, this event is here to give them an opportunity to play and win different prizes.
It's snakes and ladders but with a special twist.
Good knowledge allows the player to climb a ladder and get closer to the finish, while bad decisions make the player slide down the snake, which takes him farther from finish
So get ready to hop on the giant snake-ladder flex with the biggest dice ever!

Rules and Regulations:

▪️A team will comprise of 2 members. One to move on the flex and the other to throw the dice.
▪️Four teams will play at a time.
▪️Roll the dice to play the game.
Odd number comes: No questions, move that many steps.
Even number comes: A question will be asked, if answered correctly then move that many steps else stay where you are.
▪️To take the stairs you have to show your knowledge by answering a question.
▪️Even if you get bitten by vices you can still survive if you have luck.

Tech Charades

Silence always speaks the truth, silence is kind.

It is time when we listen to silence,
When we act and express ourselves,
When we do not use words, but SILENCE.

Tech Marathon ‘20 brings you this unique non- technical yet technical event: Tech Charades.
Bring about the actor in you and be the champion of the world of SILENCE!
So now TALK, but beware! DO NOT SPEAK.

Played in a team of 2 among which one acts the technical terms and other has to guess it correctly.

Rounds:

1. Prelims: Daily used friendly technical words will be given. One has to guess maximum no. of words in 2 mins.
2. Final: A very easy question will be asked from categories - Bollywood, Proverbs in Hindi, General Knowledge and Sci-fi.One of the team member has to answer the question and
